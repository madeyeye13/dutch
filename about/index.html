DUTCH AND DOYENNE EVENTS

Who We Are

Dutch and Doyenne Events Decoration Company is an event decoration company based in Abuja, Nigeria. Founded on the 10th of June 2018.

With over 200 satisfied clients our event decoration company continues to soar in the city of Abuja. Dutch and Doyenne Events operates majorly in two cities which is Abuja and Kaduna and we are also available to travel to other cities within and outside Nigeria.

Overtime, Dutch and Doyenne Events gained a reputation which is due to our integrity when it comes to meeting our deliverables and even exceeding it. We have processes, structure, action plans, timelines, and team building strategies that helps the company offer excellent services to our clients.

FAITH ESSIEN | FOUNDER & MD

With 6 years of experience in the event industry, Faith Essien has honed her skills in creating breathtaking and memorable events, from weddings and corporate gatherings to social soirees and galas. Her dedication to excellence and commitment to delivering exceptional results has earned Dutch and Doyenne Events a reputation for excellence in Abuja's event scene.
